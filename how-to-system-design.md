# How To System Design

## Approach

- Look for the problem statement in the specs.
    - Example: "Unified AI/ML framework"
- Context of the Company: _What do they do?_, _What do they want?_ (or looking to adopt), _What problems are they currently facing right now?_ (usually in the spec/context)
    - Example: Emerson
        - [CONTEXT] What do they do? = automation software (control systems, etc.), help other companies reach their sustainability goals
        - [PROBLEM] What problems right now? = no AI/ML unified framework, no fast way to adapt AI for teams
        - [SOLUTION - want] What do they want? = easy way to spin up new AI projects/models (for fast AI adoption)
